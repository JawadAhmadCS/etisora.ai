Questions answered

Everything you need to know.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

None at all. We handle everything — design, build, integrations and deployment. You review and approve. Once live, you manage it from a simple dashboard, or we manage it as part of your monthly growth partnership.

How long does it take to go live?

Most clients are live within 2–4 weeks. The timeline depends on integrations needed and how quickly you can review feedback. We've deployed in as little as 7 days for straightforward use cases. All plans include our 30-day go-live guarantee.

What does the monthly partnership include?

Continuous improvement — not just maintenance. Every month we review conversation data, optimise underperforming flows, expand the knowledge base, add new capabilities and hold a strategy call. Your agent compounds in value the longer we work together.

What if the agent doesn't know an answer?

Every agent is built with fallback flows — it either collects the user's information to follow up, or escalates seamlessly to a human via email, CRM ticket or live chat. Nothing gets dropped. Monthly reviews add answers to anything the agent couldn't handle.

What tools and platforms do you integrate with?

Salesforce, HubSpot, Calendly, Google Calendar, Shopify, Zendesk, Intercom, WhatsApp, Twilio, Slack and more. Custom API integrations available on Growth and Enterprise plans.

Is my customer data safe?

Yes. All etisora agents run on secure, GDPR-compliant infrastructure. We never sell or share your data. For healthcare and legal clients, we discuss compliant deployment options on your strategy call.

What makes etisora different from other AI companies?

Three things: we build custom solutions — not templates you configure yourself. We go beyond AI to close the full revenue loop with digital marketing. And you deal directly with the people building your solution — not a support queue.
